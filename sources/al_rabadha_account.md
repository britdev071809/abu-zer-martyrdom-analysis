## Overview
The widely accepted account states that Hazrat Abu Zer Ghaffari (may Allah be pleased with him) died in Al-Rabadha, a desert settlement near Medina, during the caliphate of Uthman ibn Affan (r. 644–656 CE). This narrative is supported by numerous early Islamic historians and biographers.
